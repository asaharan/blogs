How I watch IPL for free on Google Chrome
It is easy, just scroll down
Hotstar allows me to watch IPL free for 10 minutes on every browser
So I add a new user every 10 minutes to Google Chrome
How to add a new user?
  • Click your name on top corner of Google Chrome
  • Click Manage People
  • Click ADD PERSON
  • Now click ADD
  • Now open the hotstar and watch for 10 minutes
You don't need to log into Google Chrome every time you create a new user
Enjoy :)
